Question: UH POLICY: Official Campus Domain Names
Article Text: The chancellors of the 10 University of Hawaii campuses decided in 2002 and subsequently reaffirmed standardization of campus domain names as follows:manoa.hawaii.eduhilo.hawaii.eduwestoahu.hawaii.eduhawaii.hawaii.eduhonolulu.hawaii.edukapiolani.hawaii.edukauai.hawaii.eduleeward.hawaii.edumaui.hawaii.eduwindward.hawaii.edu Campuses are strongly encouraged to use the above naming convention to represent their respective campus home page on all print and electronic media. The University of Hawaii System's official website is www.hawaii.edu. Questions regarding this policy should be directed to webhead@hawaii.edu.
