Question: Creating a filter for Google@UH Calendar reminders
Article Text: You may notice that your Google@UH calendar email reminders are going into your Spam folder. To prevent this, you need to create a mail filter. Login at http://gmail.hawaii.edu Click on Settings (near the upper right corner). Depending on your browser, you may have to click on the gear-wheel icon in the upper right and select Mail Settings. Click on the Filters link Click on Create a new filter In the "From:" field, type calendar-notification@google.com Click on Next Step Check the box for Never send it to Spam Click on Create Filter Your Google@UH calendar email reminders should now be delivered to your Inbox.
