Question: Getting Started with Google@UH Groups
Article Text: The Google Groups for Apps Education Edition does not have all of the features of the standard Google Groups. The Google Groups for Apps Education Edition does not support file upload/download and does not support the creation of websites. File upload/download is a feature of Google@UH Docs while website creation is a feature of Google@UH Sites.
