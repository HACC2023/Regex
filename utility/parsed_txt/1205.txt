Question: Which web browsers are supported for Google@UH Gmail?
Article Text: Web browsers supported by Google includes, but is not limited to: FireFox 2.0 and higher Internet Explorer 6.0 and higher Safari 3.0 and higher Other supported browsers Please note that even though Google's browser support is extensive, ITS can only officially support the browsers listed here. If you experience problems viewing Gmail in your browser please try HTML view. HTML view is optimal for older unsupported browsers. HTML view is missing key features of Gmail like chat, spell checker, and colored labels. To turn on HTML view, click on the link that says basic HTML at the bottom of screen while you are logged into Gmail. Click here to learn more about HTML view.
