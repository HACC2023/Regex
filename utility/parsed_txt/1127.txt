Question: Add and remove tools in a Laulima course space
Article Text: Laulima has a variety of tools that can be added to your class or project site to facilitate communication and interaction between students and instructors. These tools show up on the left side of every course space and can be added by following the tutorial link below. This tutorial can help guide you through the process: http://www.hawaii.edu/talent/laulimatutorials/laulima_tools_usage.htm (link opens in new window)
