Question: As a parent or guardian how do I pay the tuition for my son/daughter online?
Article Text: Parents and guardians who have been set up as an Authorized User may logon to: https://epay.hawaii.edu/C24372_tsa/web/login.jsp with their email address and password. Information about being setup as an Authorized User can be found on the FAQs for Student eCommerce Services page. Information about non-online payment options can be found at the Tuition and Fee Payments page of the Bursar's Office website.
