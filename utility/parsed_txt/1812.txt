Question: Using a Digital Signature in Acrobat DC
Article Text: If you have not created a digital ID in Acrobat DC, please refer to https://www.hawaii.edu/askus/1810. Using digital ID to sign PDFs Open the form-fillable PDF and fill out all fields prior to filling out the signature. (if changes are made after signing, the document will display a note that indicates changes were made after signing) Click on the signature field. Select the digital ID you want to use for signing and click Continue. Enter the password and click on Sign. You'll be prompted to save the signed PDF, update the file name accordingly and click on Save.
