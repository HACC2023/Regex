Question: How Do I Create/Edit a Google@UH Drive document?
Article Text: To create a new document If you are logged in to Google@UH mail (http://gmail.hawaii.edu), click on Drive in the upper left-hand corner. Otherwise, visit http://gdocs.hawaii.edu directly. Click on the red Create button and select the document type (i.e., Document, Presentation, Spreadsheet, Form, Drawing, etc.). For information on editing a Google document consult the following Getting Started Guides Document https://support.google.com/drive/bin/answer.py?hl=en&topic=21008&answer=143206&rd=2 Spreadsheet https://support.google.com/drive/bin/answer.py?hl=en&topic=20322&answer=140784&rd=2 Presentation https://support.google.com/drive/bin/answer.py?hl=en&topic=19431&answer=126127&rd=2
