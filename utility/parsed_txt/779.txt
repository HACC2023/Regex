Question: Thunderbird Information (PC)
Article Text: General Information Thunderbird, a free email application, allows you to receive, send, and read your electronic mail on your computer. Minimum System Requirements A PC running Windows 10 or later An Intel Pentium 4 or newer (Core i5, 3 GHz or faster recommended) 1 GB RAM (4 GB recommended) 200 MB hard disk space Note: while 32-bit and 64-bit versions of Windows can be used to run Thunderbird, only 32-bit builds of Thunderbird are supported at this time.
