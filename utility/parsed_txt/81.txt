Question: Mapping to a Network Drive
Article Text: Windows To map a network share drive on Windows 10, complete the following steps: In the start menu, type and select "This PC" Click on the "Computer" tab and select "Map Network Drive" Click on "Browse" to locate the shared drive you would like to map macOS To map to a network share drive on macOS, complete the following steps: On your desktop, click on the "Go" menu at the top and select "Connect to Server..." If you know the address of the network drive, you may type it into the "Server Address" field and click "Connect" to connect to it directly or you can click on "Browse>" to see all the public drives on the network. If you selected "Browse" in the previous step, you can select your network drive from the list that appears.
