Telecommunications Forms

The Telecom forms are now online in Kuali. When you click on the link for the form, you will be redirected to a UH login page where you will need to login using your UH ID. Once you complete and submit the form online, the form will be routed to the next person for approval. The next person will receive an email from Kuali Notificaions with a link to review and electronically approve the request.

If you are having trouble with these forms, please contact the Telecom office by calling 956-6033 (extension 66033 from a campus line) or email us at telecom@hawaii.edu.
