BILLING POLICY

Since July 1, 2001, ITS Telecommunications ceased accepting federally funded accounts to pay for monthly recurring Telecom equipment and rental charges. Thus, federally funded accounts cannot be charged or billed by ITS Telecommunications for Telecom Equipment provided by us. Please see memo for details.
