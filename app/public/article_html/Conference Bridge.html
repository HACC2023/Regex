Conference Bridge

What is the Polycom Conference Bridge?

    The Polycom Conference Bridge is a fully integrated, all-digital, audio conference bridge that allows people located on the neighbor islands, on the mainland US, or in another country to communicate with each other, as long as one of the conference bridge participants is a faculty or staff of one of the campuses across the UH system.

To Schedule a Conference Bridge

    Email telecom@hawaii.edu with the following information:

    Department Name
    Contact Phone Number
    Date of Conference Bridge
    Start Time of Conference Bridge
    End Time of Conference Bridge
    Number of Participants

    If you have any questions, call us at 956-6033 or email us at telecom@hawaii.edu
