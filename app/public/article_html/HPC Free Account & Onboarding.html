HPC Account Request

Thanks for your interest! Using your UH account, please fill out the form linked below.

You will be asked to register for an HPC onboarding session. Training materials for that session may be viewed here - you may want to revisit these in future. A link to this material is also provided at every HPC login.

In the form below, if you see "You need permission," then you need to log out of your personal account, or open an incognito or private window, and then try viewing this form. It will require you to login on a gold screen with your UH credentials. If you still have issues please contact uh-hpc-help@lists.hawaii.edu.
